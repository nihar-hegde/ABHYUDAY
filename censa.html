Chief Guest

Mr. ASHWIN L SHETTY

He is a NATO Certified Engineer and Defence Expert, Glider Pilot, Scuba Divider, globetrotter, Rotarian, Toastmaster, Social worker, Educationist, Soft skill developer. Also, he is an arecanut, cashew and coconut farmer with a gau-shala of 30 cows and 15 sheeps. He has worked in 8 different countries for over 15years. He was an Engineering Manager with Airbus, Military division. Currently he is an Administrator of a non profit educational institution called Vidyarashmi in Savanoor, Puttur taluk, Director of Adarsha Co-operative Society, Red Cross, Puttur, Prashanth wines and CEO of Arogya Plus rural health care startup He has a Masters Degree in Materials Engineering and Aerospace structures. His MBA in Project Management from University of Queensland, Australia was sponsored by Airbus. Currently pursuing a degree in Law, LLB at KVG Law College. He returned to India to look after his parents. He is an avid follower of the Bhagavad Gita. He is Family man with wife Rashmi and two kids Atharva and Avi.
